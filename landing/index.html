Why WorkspAIce

A desktop AI client that respects your machine and your decisions.

Local-first

Your conversations stay on your machine. No telemetry, no analytics, no hosted remote config. The app is yours.

No accounts, no gates

No sign-up, no subscription, no premium tier. Download, install, run. Every feature is available from the first launch.

Multi-provider, your keys

Ollama and LM Studio for local models, plus OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, OpenRouter, DeepSeek, SiliconFlow and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. You choose the keys.

Run it your way

Pick a local model or bring your own API key. WorkspAIce stays out of the way.
